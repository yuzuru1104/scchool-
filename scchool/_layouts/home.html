Welcome to SCCHOOL

An Educational Campaign for Low Carbon Living

About SCCHOOL

SCCHOOL (Students for Climate Change and Low Carbon Living) is an educational campaign aimed at promoting low carbon living practices within our school community. We believe in empowering future leaders to make informed choices and take action towards reducing our carbon footprint.

Through engaging activities, workshops, and initiatives, SCCHOOL strives to educate students in junior secondary (S1-S3) about the importance of sustainability and inspire them to adopt low carbon behaviors and lifestyle choices.

Our Mission

At SCCHOOL, our mission is to:

  • Encourage students and staff to adopt low carbon behaviors and lifestyle choices
  • Educate the school community about the carbon footprint and the importance of reducing greenhouse gas emissions
  • Foster a sustainable culture within the school
  • Measure and report on the impact of our actions

Get Involved

We welcome all students who are passionate about environmental sustainability and want to make a positive impact. By joining SCCHOOL, you will have the opportunity to:

  • Participate in engaging workshops and educational sessions
  • Implement practical actions to reduce carbon emissions
  • Connect with like-minded students and collaborate on low carbon projects
  • Make a difference in our school and local community

Together, we can create a greener and more sustainable future!
